Financial support
 
 

 

A limited amount of support for travel and/or registration expense is available for participants from developing countries.

Since our budget is really limited, we would like to limit the support to those who are really in need, such as students from least-developed countries. (If we have many requests, we will not be able to support each person sufficiently.)

For those who are interested in this support, please contact Dr. Taro Tezuka at tezuka@dl.kuis.kyoto-u.ac.jp before October 16th, 2006.
