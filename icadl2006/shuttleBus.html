    • from Kyoto Univ.: 8:00PM (Nov. 27), 5:30PM (Nov. 29)

We also have shuttle bus services from Kyoto University to the banquet hotel (The Garden Oriental Kyoto) and from the banquet hotel to Conference hotels. Departure time of the former service is 6:00PM, and that of the latter service is 9:00PM. Please get together at the lobby 10 minutes before the departure time.
