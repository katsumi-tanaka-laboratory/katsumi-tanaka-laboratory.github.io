COE International Symposium on Informatics Research for Knowledge Society

The International Symposium on Informatics Research for Knowledge Society offers an opportunity to discuss and exchange ideas on future informatics research for the coming knowledge society among researchers and engineers. Many famous and influential researchers are invited to give talks on the topic.
