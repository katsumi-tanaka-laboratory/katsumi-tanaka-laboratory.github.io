異文化コラボレーションワークショップ

Workshop on Intercultural Collaboration

言語、文化の違いを超えたコラボレーションが、東アジアにおいて大切な課題となりつつある。本ワークショップでは、異文化コラボレーション研究の国際的基盤を形成するために、日中韓馬秦の５か国の研究者が集まり、研究課題を提示しあい、相互に協力する体制を確立するための議論を行う。
Suppose you are motivated to start intercultural collaboration research.
Suppose you think you have interesting research issues, good students and enough research funds. You will find a lot of difficulties, however, when planning details of your research projects: how to gather machine translators among five or six languages, how to get human subjects from five to six different countries, how to compare your data and those from other experiments, and so on. Toestablish research basis for intercultural collaboration, this workshop tries to share not only research issues but also machine translators, servers, statistical data, and human subjects in different countries.
